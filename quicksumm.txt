Billionaire inventor Tony Stark, known for his weapons manufacturing, was captured by terrorists in Afghanistan. Forced to build a weapon, he instead created the Iron Man suit to escape. Returning home, Tony renounced weapons production and became Iron Man, a superhero fighting crime and terrorism. He uncovers a conspiracy by his business partner, Obadiah Stane, who had been selling weapons to terrorists and orchestrated his capture. Tony defeats Stane and publicly reveals his identity, dedicating himself to protecting the world as Iron Man.
